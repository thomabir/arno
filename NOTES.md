# Notes

## Ideas for features

- Can be remote controlled, configured, and monitored via phone
- Manual overrides: water now (mL amount setable, date/time setable), do not water (optional end date)
- manual schedule (eg. 500 mL every week)
- don't water during certain times (at night etc.)
- Auto mode: How long to wait after plant is dry, how much water
- After watering, pump back again so there is no stale water in the tube?
  - Then have to know how much water volume there is in the tube, can estimate from length
- sensible default failure settings:
  - never water while the upper reservoir sensor is wet
  - Minimum interval: don't water within one hour of having watered
- Tracking
  - Track amount of water consumed per plant and total
- only operate one pump at a time
- can resume operation correctly after power failure
